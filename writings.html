About My Writings

In addition to my Mathematics major, I am also pursuing a Creative Writing minor. To be totally honest, my main reason for pursuing the minor was because the minor is relatively short, and the classes required nicely covered my weird gen-ed requirements. It's basically a free minor. However, I am extremely grateful for having taking this track, as I have produced many works that I am very proud of. Much like the games I have made, these works may not be extremely complex or especially polished, but I think that they are really funny, so I love them anyways.

America's Great Depression

February - March 2022

While not the funniest thing I've ever written, this piece is why I pursued a Creative Writing minor. My professor enjoyed the humor of my work, my transformation of a typical college essay into entertainment, and suggested that I pursue a Creative Writing minor, so I looked into it and said "why not?"

This paper was written for a required first-year writing course called "Writing about Public Problems," and I decided to write about a mundane local issue instead of a greater societal issue that I would not have had any real authority over. My mom loves this paper.
